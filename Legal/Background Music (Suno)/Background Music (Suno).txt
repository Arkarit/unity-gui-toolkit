Demo Background Music
=====================

The background music tracks in the demo project
(.Dev-App/Unity/Assets/Music/) were generated with Suno (https://suno.com)
under a Suno Pro plan (account: phoenix616).

Under the Suno Pro plan the generating account holds the commercial-use rights
to the generated songs. They may therefore be used commercially alongside this
(commercially usable) library.

Scope: These songs are part of the DEMO PROJECT only. They are NOT included in
the distributed unity-gui-toolkit package. This note exists for provenance and
to make the commercial-use situation explicit.
